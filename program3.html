Summary

Yoga is a comprehensive practice that encompasses physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), meditation, and a philosophical approach to life. Its origins trace back to ancient India, aiming to unite the body, mind, and spirit123.

Boosts mental health: Yoga can alleviate stress, anxiety, and depression4. Promotes heart health: It may lower blood pressure and reduce the risk of heart disease5.
